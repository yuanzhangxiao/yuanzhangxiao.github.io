Short Bio

I am an assistant professor at University of Hawaii at Manoa.

Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral fellow in Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Northwestern University, working with Prof. Ermin Wei. At Northwestern University, I am also fortunate to work with Prof. Randall Berry at EECS and Prof. Chaithanya Bandi at Kellogg School of Management.

I got my Ph.D. from Department of Electrical Engineering, UCLA in 2014. My advisor was Prof. Mihaela van der Schaar. At UCLA, I was also fortunate to work with Prof. William Zame at Department of Economics and Prof. Florian Dörfler at ETH Zurich.

I received my B.E degree and M.S. degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in 2006 and 2009, respectively. My M.S. advisor was Prof. Shidong Zhou.
